FAQ

What is Vpnufo?

Vpnufo is a community of users who make information on the Internet available to each other. In other words, Vpnufo is a Peer-to-
Peer VPN that is ad-free and free of charge.
Will my information be available to other users in the community?
Definitely not. Vpnufo handles your HTTP requests in browsing mode, the same way as a regular Internet service provider.



Why is Vpnufo free?

You do not pay for the plan because you are trading some of your device's resources (Wi-Fi and very limited cellular data) for a
Vpnufo proxy. We use a special network for HTTP, with which you can access the sites you need through the devices of other Vpnufo
users, and not through expensive servers.
